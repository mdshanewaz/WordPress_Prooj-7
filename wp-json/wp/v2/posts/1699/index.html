1. Decide on Your Focus for the Sports Blog<\/strong><\/h3>\n

<\/p>\n

Your first step is to spend a bit of time thinking about the type of sports blog you want to create. The reason why you need to decide on a specific focus for your sports blog is that\u00a0\u2018sports\u2019\u00a0<\/em>is such an extensive topic that if you start there, it\u2019s unlikely to do well.<\/p>\n

Merely having sports as the theme of your blog is way too large a subject for you to handle on your own, especially at the beginning. There are just too many sports that you could talk about, and too many different elements of each sport to dive into.<\/p>\n

It would be impossible for you to be able to focus on every one of them and your audience would be far too large.<\/p>\n

A better way to start your sports blog is to focus on one area that you can cover. It\u2019s best if you choose an area where you have previous knowledge and a firm understanding of the players, history, rules, techniques, player transactions, etc.<\/p>\n

Ask yourself questions such as:<\/p>\n

Will my blog be about baseball or football?\u00a0<\/em><\/p>\n

Will it cover major leagues or local college football?\u00a0<\/em><\/p>\n

Do I want to focus on a local team or an entire league?<\/em><\/p>\n

Here are some ideas to get the ball rolling on your brainstorming process:<\/strong><\/p>\n

    \n
  • You can focus on a local sports team (college, professional, or even high school).<\/li>\n
  • Write about the athletes of the teams including their upbringing and accolades.<\/li>\n
  • Write about the latest sport or athlete news.<\/li>\n
  • Blog about your favorite sports stadiums.<\/li>\n
  • Share information about your favorite team(s).<\/li>\n
  • Cover an entire major league (NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, etc.)<\/li>\n
  • Create articles and reviews of sports clothing and equipment.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    There is no right or wrong when it comes to choosing a focus for your blog.\u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n

    However, it\u2019s important to choose something that you will enjoy writing about on a consistent basis for months and years to come.<\/p>\n

    You should also note that you can always change your focus later, so pick whatever feels right and move on to the next step.<\/p>\n
